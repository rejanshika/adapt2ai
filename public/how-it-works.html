Practical Pacing

The Engagement Timeline

We structure our process to eliminate assumptions, proving the value of custom AI systems prior to full deployment.

  • 01 Discovery Call
  • 02 AI Readiness
  • 03 Strategy
  • 04 Working MVP
  • 05 Implementation
  • 06 Scale
01 / SCOPING

Discovery Call

We kick off with a free 30-minute scoping call to study your business structure, department bottlenecks, and database siloes. No preparation is required — we focus on understanding your current operational friction.

02 / AUDIT

AI Readiness

An initial audit mapping your existing software integrations, database architectures, and task workflows. We filter the highest-impact opportunities based on technical feasibility and business ROI.

03 / ROADMAP

Strategy

For organizations requiring complex multi-departmental rollouts, we build custom security compliance bounds, database migration plans, and model selection roadmaps to outline safe, scalable deployments.

04 / THE CORE PROOF

Working MVP

We compile and host a custom, functioning MVP sandbox around your chosen operational bottleneck. We demonstrate the tool in action, handling your data before any licensing or final pricing agreements begin.

Live Prototype
05 / ENGINEERING

Implementation

Once the MVP verifies viability, we scale it to production. We configure production API endpoints, establish data security protocols, and integrate custom LLMs or workflow agents into your operational stack.

06 / GROWTH

Scale

Continuous monitoring, integration tuning, and staff onboarding audits. We train your internal team so you own and manage the software infrastructure directly without locked-in consultant dependency.
